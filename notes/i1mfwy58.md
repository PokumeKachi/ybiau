# Advanced Problem

## Problem

- Given that the equation $(log_2x)^2-log_2x-2025m=0$ has two distinct solutions $x_1$ and $x_2$, and the equation $9^y-m\cdot3^y+(\sqrt{3})^7=0$ has two distinct solutions $y_1$ and $y_2$, such that the two points $A(x_1,y_1)$ and $B(x_2,y_2)$ on the $Oxy$ plane satisfy $OA\perp OB$. Find $m$.

## Solution

- $OA\perp OB\Leftrightarrow\overrightarrow{OA}\cdot\overrightarrow{OB}\Leftrightarrow x_1x_2+y_1y_2=0\quad(1)$

- Using Vieta's formulas:

    $log_2x_1+log_2x_2=\frac{-b}{a}=1$

    $\Rightarrow log_2x_1x_2=1=log_22$

    $\Rightarrow x_1x_2=2\quad(2)$
