# Classical Mechanics

## Kinematics

- Distance ($s$): The total length of the traveled path of motion
- Displacement ($\overrightarrow{d}$): A vector pointing from the start of the motion to the end of motion
- Speed: $v=\frac{ds}{dt}$
- Velocity: $\overrightarrow{v}=\frac{d\overrightarrow{d}}{dt}$
- Acceleration: $\overrightarrow{a} = \frac{d\overrightarrow{v}}{dt} = \frac{d^2\overrightarrow{d}}{dt^2}$
